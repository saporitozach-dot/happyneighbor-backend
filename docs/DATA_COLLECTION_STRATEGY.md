# Data Collection Strategy for Happy Neighbor

## Overview
This document outlines strategies for collecting neighborhood data for Boston and St. Louis metro areas.

## Recommended Approach: Multi-Channel Strategy

### 1. **Self-Service Resident Submissions** ⭐ (Highest Priority)
**What:** Allow residents to submit neighborhood surveys directly through the app
**Why:** Most scalable, builds community engagement, low cost
**How:**
- Add "Submit Your Neighborhood" feature on homepage
- Create dedicated submission page for each neighborhood
- Send email campaigns to residents
- Share on social media

**Implementation:**
- Already have admin panel for manual entry
- Can add public-facing submission form
- Consider adding email verification for submissions

### 2. **Community Partnerships** ⭐⭐
**Target Partners:**
- **Neighborhood Associations** (HOAs, Civic Groups)
  - Contact: Wellesley Hills Association, Lexington Community Center, etc.
  - Offer: Free neighborhood profiles, recognition
- **Real Estate Agents**
  - Offer: Featured listings, neighborhood insights
  - They have deep local knowledge
- **Local Facebook Groups / Nextdoor**
  - Post in: "Wellesley MA Community", "St. Louis Neighborhoods"
  - Create dedicated posts with survey links
- **Community Centers & Libraries**
  - Partner for community events
  - Set up information booths

**Outreach Template:**
```
Subject: Help Build Your Neighborhood Profile on Happy Neighbor

Hi [Neighborhood Name] Residents,

We're building a platform to help people find neighborhoods that match their lifestyle. We'd love your input about [Neighborhood Name]!

Take 2 minutes to share what makes your neighborhood special:
[Link to survey]

Your responses help future neighbors make informed decisions.
```

### 3. **Incentivized Submissions** ⭐
**Options:**
- **Small Incentives:** $5-10 gift cards for first 50 submissions per neighborhood
- **Recognition:** "Verified Resident" badges, featured profiles
- **Gamification:** Leaderboards, points system
- **Early Access:** Beta features for active contributors

**Cost Estimate:**
- 125 neighborhoods × 10 submissions = 1,250 submissions
- At $5 each = $6,250 total
- Or focus on top 20 neighborhoods = $1,000

### 4. **Targeted Door-to-Door** (Selective)
**When to Use:**
- For high-value neighborhoods (Wellesley, Ladue, etc.)
- When you need initial seed data
- For neighborhoods with low online presence

**Strategy:**
- Focus on neighborhood association leaders first
- Target long-term residents (10+ years)
- Use mobile-friendly survey on tablet
- Offer small incentive ($5 coffee gift card)

**Time Estimate:**
- 10-15 minutes per house
- 20 houses per neighborhood = 3-4 hours
- For 20 neighborhoods = 60-80 hours

### 5. **Public Data Sources** (Supplement)
**Free Data:**
- **Census Data:** Demographics, income levels
- **Walk Score API:** Walkability scores
- **Crime Statistics:** Local police department data
- **School Ratings:** GreatSchools.org, Niche.com
- **Transit Data:** Public transportation access

**Implementation:**
- Can be automated via APIs
- Provides baseline data
- Residents can verify/refine

### 6. **Social Media & Online Communities**
**Platforms:**
- **Facebook Groups:**
  - "Wellesley MA Community"
  - "St. Louis Neighborhoods"
  - "Boston Suburbs"
- **Nextdoor:** Direct neighborhood targeting
- **Reddit:**
  - r/boston
  - r/StLouis
  - r/Wellesley
- **Local Forums:** Town-specific discussion boards

**Content Strategy:**
- Create engaging posts with neighborhood questions
- Share interesting neighborhood insights
- Build community around the platform

## Sample Outreach Email

```
Subject: Help Build Your Neighborhood Profile - [Neighborhood Name]

Hi [Name],

I'm reaching out from Happy Neighbor, a platform helping people find neighborhoods that match their lifestyle.

We're building profiles for neighborhoods in [Boston/St. Louis] and would love your input about [Neighborhood Name]!

As a resident, you know your neighborhood best. Would you take 2 minutes to share:
- What's the noise level like?
- How social is the community?
- Are there regular events or cookouts?
- Is it family-friendly?
- Walkability?

[Link to Survey]

Your responses help future neighbors make informed decisions. We're also offering [incentive if applicable] for the first 10 responses.

Thank you!
[Your Name]
Happy Neighbor Team
```

## Metrics to Track

- **Submissions per neighborhood:** Target 5-10 minimum
- **Response rate:** Track email open rates, click-through
- **Data quality:** Review submissions for completeness
- **Engagement:** Track return visitors, shares

- Time investment: 10-20 hours/week

**Moderate Investment:**

- Paid social ads: $500/month
- Total: ~$3,000-4,000

**Full Campaign:**
- Incentives: $5,000-10,000
- Paid ads: $2,000/month

- Total: ~$15,000-20,000
